Weight Loss Workout List

  • Cardiovascular exercises:
    • 30 minutes of brisk walking or jogging
    • 20 minutes of cycling
    • 15 minutes of jump rope
  • Strength training exercises:
    • 10 push-ups
    • 10 squats
    • 10 lunges
    • 10 dumbbell curls
    • 10 tricep dips
  • Interval training:
    • 30 seconds of high-intensity jumping jacks
    • 30 seconds of rest
    • 30 seconds of burpees
    • 30 seconds of rest
    • Repeat for 10 minutes

Weight Loss Diet Plan

Breakfast

  • 1 cup of oatmeal with berries
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 slice of whole-grain toast
  • 1 cup of green tea

Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 small apple
  • 10 almonds

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup of brown rice
  • 1 small salad with olive oil dressing

Afternoon Snack

  • Greek yogurt with sliced cucumber

Dinner

  • Grilled fish (salmon, tilapia, or cod)
  • Steamed asparagus
  • Quinoa salad with tomatoes and cucumber

Evening Snack

  • Carrot sticks with hummus

Remember to drink plenty of water throughout the day and listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.

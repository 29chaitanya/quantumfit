Muscle Gain Diet Plan

Breakfast

  • 2-3 whole eggs
  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  • 1 piece of fruit

Mid-Morning Snack

  • Protein shake made with milk or water
  • 1 small handful of mixed nuts
  • 1 small handful of dried fruits

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast or lean beef
  • 1 cup of brown rice or quinoa
  • 1-2 cups of mixed vegetables
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil or avocado oil dressing

Afternoon Snack

  • 1 can of tuna in water
  • 6-8 whole-grain crackers
  • 1 cup of raw vegetables (carrot sticks, cucumber slices)

Pre-Workout Snack

  • 1 small banana
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

Post-Workout Meal

  • Protein shake made with milk or water
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 cup of steamed broccoli

Dinner

  • Grilled salmon or tofu
  • 1 cup of quinoa or whole-wheat pasta
  • 1-2 cups of roasted vegetables
  • 1 side salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette dressing

Evening Snack

  • 1 cup of cottage cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 small handful of almonds

Remember to drink plenty of water throughout the day and adjust portion sizes to suit your caloric and macronutrient needs.

Weight Gain Workout List

  • Compound exercises:
    • Barbell squats - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Deadlifts - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Bench press - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Overhead press - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Isolation exercises:
    • Dumbbell bicep curls - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
    • Tricep pushdowns - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
    • Shoulder lateral raises - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
    • Leg curls - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Core exercises:
    • Plank - Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute, repeat 3 times
    • Russian twists - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
    • Crunches - 3 sets of 10-15 reps

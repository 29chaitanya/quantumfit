Weight Gain Diet Plan

Breakfast

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of whole milk

Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 serving of Greek yogurt
  • 1 handful of mixed nuts
  • 1 piece of fruit

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup of quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 cup of roasted vegetables
  • 1 glass of fruit juice

Afternoon Snack

  • Protein shake made with milk or water
  • 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  • 1 handful of dried fruits

Dinner

  • Salmon fillet
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 cup of steamed broccoli
  • 1 side salad with avocado and olive oil dressing

Evening Snack

  • 1 cup of cottage cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 handful of granola

Remember to drink plenty of water throughout the day and adjust portion sizes to suit your caloric needs.
